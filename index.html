Ganesh Sivaraman

Assistant Professor, Department of Materials Design & Innovation, SUNY Buffalo

My group is pioneering a next-generation atomistic modelling research program, distinguished by its fusion of scientific machine learning and experimental data integration. Our goal is to learn physics-informed models that go beyond traditional approaches to atomistic simulations, with applications spanning materials characterization and material design for personalized medicine.
