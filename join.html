Desired Skills

Interest and Adaptability

A strong interest in statistical mechanics and interdisciplinary computational modeling. Prior experience in computational modelling is not required — enthusiasm for learning is what matters most.

Programming

Proficiency in Python, with experience in scientific libraries such as NumPy, SciPy, and Pandas.

Machine Learning Frameworks

Familiarity with JAX or PyTorch is preferred. Experience with Equinox/JAX implementations is a bonus.

Computational Chemistry

While experience in density functional theory (DFT) calculations, statistical mechanics, molecular simulations, or chemical physics is highly desirable, it is not mandatory for students eager to learn — just as I transitioned into this field from a mechanical engineering background.

Preferred Background

We encourage students with degrees in mathematics, physics, chemistry, materials science, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, computer science, or related fields to apply. Experience in deep learning is desirable but not required.

What We Look For

We value curiosity, passion for interdisciplinary research, and strong problem-solving skills. If you are excited to tackle challenging problems in computational modelling, we want to hear from you!
